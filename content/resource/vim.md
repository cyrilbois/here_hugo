## note to self
**how to highlight**:
- `v` is visual mode
- `Shift v` is visual line mode.

- there's 3 options to **exit insert mode**, `<esc>`, `Ctrl + C`, and `Ctrl + [` 
- `w` & `b` are compadres
- use these keys, plus the movements & yankind & pasting stuff I've learnt till they feel like second nature.
